# DSC-520-Assignment
Task#3:
The number of downloads of different band's songs on iTune: Discrete as the downloads can be any whole number including a "zero" for no download.
The names of the bands downloaded: Nominal variable as there can be multiple names. 
The position in the itunes download chart: Ordinal value as the position in the chart has an order.
The money earned by the bands from the downloads: continuous and ratio as the the money earned can get a distinct score and also could have fractions as well.
Weight of drungs bought by the band with their royalties: continuous and ratio as the weight could be a whole number or it could be fractions as well.
The type of drugs bought by the band with royalties: Nominal variables as there can be multiple drug names.
The phone numbers that the bands obtained because of their fame: continuous as the numbers are always distinct/whole and not fractions.
The gender of the people giving the bands their phone number: Binary (assuming there are only two kinds of gender present).
The instruments played by the band members: categorical and nominal as there are different musical instruments but they fall into various category.
The time they had spent learning the instrument: Continuous
